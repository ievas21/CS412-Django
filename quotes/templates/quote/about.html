One of Mexico's greatest painters, Frida Kahlo was born on July 6, 1907 in Mexico City, Mexico.

Kahlo was best known for her self-portraits that depict themes such as identity, death, nature, and the human body. Her work was best described as surrealism.

By the early 1990s, Kahlo was not only a recgonized figure in art history, but she had also become an icon for the feminist movement.
